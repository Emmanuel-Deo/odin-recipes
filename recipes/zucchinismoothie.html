Zucchini Smoothie

TThis zucchini smoothie is a blend of orange juice, banana, and fresh zucchini that makes a great-tasting breakfast. And don't worry — you can't even taste the zucchini in it!

Enjoy !

Ingredients

  • ½ cup ice cubes, or as needed (Optional)
  • ½ zucchini, shredded
  • ½ frozen banana
  • ½ cup orange juice

Steps

  1. Combine ice cubes, zucchini, banana, and orange juice in a blender..
  2. Blend until smooth.
